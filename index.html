The Hotel

A sanctuary of comfort, crafted for the discerning traveller.

Le Pavé Residences is a refined furnished-apartment concept set in the serene hills of Ain Aar. Each residence and suite opens onto a spacious private terrace with uninterrupted views over the mountains and Beirut's shimmering coastline.

Conceived as a true reflection of modern luxury with an eco-conscious spirit, Le Pavé blends architectural elegance with the calm of nature — a wellness-driven retreat where every detail is considered.

  • 24-hour reception & security
  • Indoor & outdoor parking
  • Seaside & mountain terraces
  • 10 minutes from Beirut
